About Batumi Island Estates

A specialised luxury subsidiary of Batumi Estates, dedicated to premium island and waterfront properties on the Batumi Islands.

Founded in 2022, Batumi Estates began with a vision to transform Georgia’s real estate landscape by offering global investors transparent guidance, trusted insights, and luxury-focused portfolios. Today, the brand proudly serves clients across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and the Americas, establishing itself as a leading investment partner for high-growth Georgian developments.

In 2024, with the Georgian government initiating its historic luxury island megaproject in Batumi, we expanded our mission by launching our premium subsidiary — Batumi Island Estates. This specialized arm exclusively focuses on luxury island residences, waterfront apartments, and high-yield investment assets within the iconic man-made Batumi Islands. Through our close alignment with the project’s development roadmap, we provide early-access opportunities, curated premium units, and a seamless buying experience tailored for global investors.

In 2024 alone, we successfully assisted 100+ clients in securing high-potential coastal and island properties — offering end-to-end service, legal facilitation, documentation support, and personalized investment guidance. Clients consistently commend our prompt communication, clear advisory, and effortless property purchase process in Georgia. As the Batumi Islands evolve into the Black Sea’s most prestigious address, we remain dedicated to delivering unmatched excellence and value for every investor.
